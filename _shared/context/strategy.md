## NSLS 2025-2026: Building Tomorrow While Delivering Today
Version 1.1
Executive Summary: NSLS will transform from a traditional honor society with leadership certifications into a personalized success society while maintaining our $35.4M foundational revenue across our college partnerships. Through adding Ignite, which can serve NSLS members and those not currently invited into NSLS, we'll both solidify our core business and expand our addressable market from 4M to 20M members.

### Strategic Imperative
The ONE Priority: Launch and prove the Ignite product offering and technology while sustaining and improving core program excellence.
North Star Vision: The NSLS is a purpose-led global community of 25 million members driven toward personal and professional success and inspiring meaningful change in the world through the philosophy, 'Together We Achieve More Than We Do Alone.'

### Business Model Transformation
From "Honor Society for Top Students" → "Success Society for Everyone"
- Today: We're an honor society that only invites students with top GPAs (serving 4M eligible students)
- Tomorrow: We keep the honor society AND launch Ignite—a career success product available to ALL students (20M+ market)
- The Bridge: Students using Ignite who hit certain success milestones can earn their invitation to NSLS, even without the GPA requirement
- Why This Matters: We 5x our addressable market while keeping our core honor society strong
From "One-Time Experience / One-Time Fee" → "Lifetime Success Partner"
- Today: Students join, complete steps, get inducted, and we're mostly done with them
- Tomorrow: We become their lifetime success society through:
  - AI that learns their goals and personalizes guidance
  - Connections to community peers to support and members who've walked similar paths, offering real advice
  - Continuous support for each new career and leadership milestone (career direction → school leadership position → first job → promotion → career pivot → leadership roles)
- Why This Matters: Creates the possibility of ongoing weekly active usage, enabling the possibility of a subscription model with higher lifetime value, stronger retention, and members who actually achieve their career goals

### Sequenced Business Objectives

#### Phase 1: Foundation (Completed)
- Revamped Core Product Experience [Product]
  - What: Redesigned FOL curriculum and SNT matching system
  - Impact: Demonstrate core product commitment to internal staff and partners. Prototype changes to iterate towards improvement upon the baseline 20% completion rate to inform future improvements
- Built Data & Analytics Infrastructure [Marketing, Product and Tech]
  - What: Implemented PostHog, Customer.io, and configured unified Customer Data Platform
  - Impact: Reduced analytics blind spots by 80%, enabling data-driven iteration vs. guesswork
- Stabilized Technical Foundation [Tech]
  - What: Migrated 70% of critical systems off legacy Drupal
  - Impact: Accelerated development velocity and reduced revenue impacting technical risk
- Defined and Aligned on Product Vision and Market Expansion Thesis [SLT]
  - What: Defined and prototyped Career + Connections value propositions
  - Impact: Enabled strategic clarity and execution focus
- De-risked AI Innovation [Tech / Ignite Team]
  - What: Designed, built and tested Ignite AI coaching with early adopters among NSLS members
  - Impact: Achieved 20% profile completion rate without any lifecycle marketing, validating technology, and personalization approach

#### Phase 2: Market Expansion (Next 12-18 Months)
- Activate Freemium Distribution Channel [Marketing, Client Services and Product]
  - Target: Increase response rate with GPA-qualified students at partner schools from 5% to 6% by allowing those who initially decline their invitation to use the Freemium version of Ignite Career offering
  - How: Allow students who don’t accept their nomination to try Career before joining NSLS, increasing conversion to enrollment by demonstrating value
- Transform Brand Architecture for Tiered Growth [Marketing, Client Services, and Product]
  - Target: Enable freemium-to-premium conversion funnel, where non-GPA qualified students can be a non-honor member of our community, then ‘earn’ their way into the Honor tier by showing progress and community contributions.
  - How: Create "Success Society" umbrella with honor as earned achievement
- Merge New Product Features, Learnings, and Capabilities into Core Product [Product]
  - Target: Increase member engagement/adoption from 20% to 27.5% completion
  - How: Integrate new product offerings, scaled national events, leveraging learning from foundations reimagined, new lifecycle communications, and AI personalization into the existing core FOL experience
- Scale Career Offering to Unlock 5X Addressable Market Growth [Marketing, Product, and Client Services]
  - Target: Expand addressable market from 4M to 20M+ by Q2 2026
  - How: Launch career coaching to invited, but not enrolled, and uninvited segments, removing GPA barriers
- Build Subscription-Ready Engagement [Product]
  - Target: 10% of members actively using the platform weekly by Q2 2026
  - How: Launch community features and optimized/ongoing coaching touchpoints
- Complete AI-Powered Transformation [Product and Tech]
  - Target: 100% of content delivered through a personalized AI platform
  - How: Sunset current LMS, fully integrate personalized learning with the leadership program, as well as all other content offerings
- Launch Subscription Revenue Stream [Client Services, Marketing, Product, and Tech]
  - Target: Test $10/month model
  - How: Align incentives - we succeed when members succeed long-term

#### Target Resource Allocation
Year-to-date resource allocation as of Aug, 2025, keeping spending limited to mitigate risk:
- 91% - Maintaining and improving core program excellence and revenue
- 9% - Building, testing, and enhancing the Ignite platform and features
We will gradually increase resource allocation as the new model is proven.

### Success Metrics Dashboard

#### 2025 Targets
- L1: Core Program Revenue: Maintain $35.2M (funds transformation)
- L1: Product Relaunch: Transform NSLS's core experience from legacy course delivery to AI-powered personalized journeys across Leadership, Career, and Connections, rolling up into a Success Community, while maintaining current performance (5% enrollment, 20% induction), with proof points showing path to subscription business model and a full percentage point enrollment conversion improvement (e.g. 5% to 6% response rate at a school) through freemium value demonstration by Q4 2025.
  - L2: Ignite Adoption: 25%+ member profile completion rate (>5,000 profiles)
  - L2: Product Release: Ignite v2 (enabling community and connections, online SNTs with two-way video, chat, AI-facilitated introductions with SNTs, etc.) available by November 15, 2025
  - L2: New product offering: Send 1M invites to invited but not enrolled leads from our partner schools in Q4 2025.

#### 2026 Targets
- Enrollment Conversion: Increase from 5% to 6% at partner schools with freemium distribution
- New Market Activation: Launch and test with 4M+ non-invited students at partner schools
- Business Model Transition: Transition brand and distribution to enable a larger addressable market and test subscription revenue model

### Strategic Boundaries

#### What We WILL Do:
- Launch Ignite with AI-powered coaching for Leadership, Career, and Connections
- Implement freemium model to lower entry barriers
- Enable non-GPA qualified students to earn honor through demonstrated progress
- Maintain all existing college partnerships and FOL quality

#### What We WON'T Do:
- Abandon core program current delivery mechanism before Ignite proves product-market fit (by exceeding adoption and satisfaction metrics vs. current product baselines)
- Risk our current college partnerships
- Pursue initiatives outside of improving our core program offering and expanding our addressable market and conversion rate with the Ignite offering

### Key Milestones & Timeline
- Q3 2025: Ignite v1.3 (market expansion) launch to NSLS members from 2024 and spring 2025 who are aged 20+
- Q4 2025: Ignite v2 (community features)
- Q4 2025: Career product release to NSLS Members who joined Fall 2025
- Q4 2025: Freemium model testing begins for non-NSLS members
- Q1 2026: Sequenced market expansion rollout
- Q2 2026: Subscription model testing

### Risk Management

#### Primary Risks:
- College Partnership Risk: Colleges may resist new distribution model
  - Mitigation: Transparent communication, pilot programs
  - Mitigation: Deep partner collaboration and co-development of two-way value proposition
- Adoption and Satisfaction Risk: Members may not engage with Ignite
  - Mitigation: Start with subset market segments and prove value before scaling
  - Mitigation: Strong analytics stack and rapid optimizations to drive funnel conversion and adoption
- Revenue Risk: Transition timing could impact cash flow
  - Mitigation: Core program funds innovation until proven
  - Mitigation: Test new subscription revenue outside of the current enrollment flow
  - Mitigation: 2025/26 focus on analytics and response rate drives additional revenue from core

### The Path Forward
We're not abandoning what works—we're amplifying it. By maintaining our honor society foundation while building a scalable success platform, we create a future where millions of students can access personalized coaching, meaningfully connect and help each other in a community for driven optimists, and earn recognition through demonstrated progress, not just grades.
Together We Achieve More Than We Do Alone—especially when we unite tradition with transformation.
