## Groups (Topgrading behavioral taxonomy)

### Intellectual
- **Strategic Skills** — determines opportunities & threats through analysis of current & future trends; big-picture.
- **Judgment / Decision Making** — consistent logic, rationality & objectivity; neither indecisive nor hip-shooter.
- **Analysis Skills** — probes all fruitful sources for answers; identifies root causes; sizes up problems quickly.
- **Creativity / Pragmatism** — generates and recognizes imaginative, workable solutions.
- **Risk Taking** — takes calculated risks with generally favorable outcomes.

### Personal
- **Organization / Planning** — plans, organizes, schedules & budgets efficiently; focuses on key priorities.
- **Integrity** — ironclad; does not compromise honesty; earns trust.
- **Adaptability** — adjusts quickly to changing priorities and conditions; copes effectively with complexity.
- **Self-Awareness** — knows own strengths/limits; seeks and acts on feedback.
- **Energy / Drive / Passion** — exhibits energy and the will to win.

### Interpersonal
- **Team Player** — reaches out to peers; overcomes we-they; leads peers to do what's best for the company.
- **Written Communications** — writes clear, precise, well-organized documents.
- **Oral / Presentation** — communicates clearly and persuasively to groups.
- **Listening** — tunes in accurately to others; hears the meaning, not just the words.
- **Conflict Management** — surfaces and resolves conflict constructively.

### Management
- **Selecting / Recruiting** — recruits and selects A-players.
- **Training / Development / Coaching** — actively trains and develops people for promotion; a people builder.
- **Goal Setting** — sets fair, stretch goals; drives accountability to them.
- **Empowerment** — pushes decision-making to the lowest level; provides authority & resources.
- **Performance Management** — measures performance and holds people accountable; addresses underperformance.

### Leadership
- **Vision** — provides clear, credible direction; frames a compelling future.
- **Change Leadership** — actively intervenes to create & energize positive change; leads by example.
- **Inspiring Followership** — builds trust and commitment; people want to follow.
- **Removing Roadblocks** — clears obstacles so the team can execute.

### Motivational
- **Ambition** — desires to grow in responsibility and impact.
- **Tenacity** — passionately persists to reach goals despite obstacles.
- **Standards** — sets and holds a high bar for self and others.

### AI Literacy (NSLS addition)
- **AI Fluency** — uses AI tools effectively to raise leverage; knows what to delegate to AI and how to verify it.

## Selection notes

- **Manager-level roles** should carry at least one people-development competency (Training/Development/Coaching, Empowerment, or an explicit Develops Others).
- Pick competencies that are **differentiating for the role** — the 5–8 that separate great from adequate in *this* seat, not a generic leadership set.
- The **Growth Focus** typically targets a competency scored below its MAR.
